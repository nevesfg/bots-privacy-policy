## Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

This privacy policy applies to all Discord bots developed and operated by **nevesfg**.

### What data we collect

Depending on the bot's functionality, we may collect:

- **Discord user IDs and tags** — to identify users within the context of bot features (e.g., tickets, purchases, commands).
- **Message content** — read in real time only when required for bot functionality (e.g., AI responses, command processing). Message content is not stored permanently unless explicitly stated.
- **Guild and channel IDs** — to associate bot configurations with the correct server and channels.
- **Guild member roles** — checked when needed to determine user permissions or staff status.

### What we do NOT do

- We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties.
- We do not collect data beyond what is necessary for the bot's features.
- We do not store message content in our databases unless required for a specific feature explicitly disclosed to users.

### Third-party services

Some bots may use third-party services to operate. These may include:

- **Groq API** — used for AI-generated responses. Data sent to Groq is processed under [Groq's Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/).
- **MongoDB** — used to store bot configuration and operational metadata in a private, access-controlled database.
- **Discord API** — all bots operate under [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).

### Data retention

We do not permanently store any user data. All data collected during the use of our bots is used solely in real time to provide the requested functionality and is not retained in any database after use.

### Contact

For any privacy concerns or data removal requests, contact the bot owner directly via Discord: **nevesfg**.
